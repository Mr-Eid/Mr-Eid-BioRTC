## About Me

I am a Research Assistant in the Genomic Unit at the Biomedical Research and Training Centre (BioRTC), Yobe State University. My work focuses on processing and extraction of genomic DNA from over 1,200 participant samples that will undergo genotyping and sequencing to investigate the contribution of APOE genotype and haplotypes to dementia risk in African populations. In parallel, i contributes to genomic surveillance of infectious diseases (i.e malaria genomics) and stem-cell related projects that increasingly depend on sequencing-based analysis.  i passionate to solve the drug‑resistant malaria death at my community in the North‑Eastern Nigeria.
